Your Host

A Fellow Seeker,
Not a Guru

Millennial Multi-Hyphenate · Digital Nomad · Decade-Long Meditator

My path has been unconventional by design, from financial analyst at a leading management consulting firm to Wall Street business transformation consultant for Fortune 500 companies in New York City. From there, I co-founded a creative agency, led a cross-functional team with a European social enterprise fund, incubated a probiotic health beverage startup, and found my companion for life in a new city.

Five years ago, I took a sabbatical and turned inward. What started as curiosity became a decade-long commitment: a 10-Day Vipassana silent retreat, a 3-month intensive Patanjali yoga and meditation program at a leading spiritual university, and annual Himalayan treks where I met sages, yogis, mystics, and monks. Meditation did not just change how I felt, it changed how I think, create, and lead.

My wife and I want to extend our home as an alternative to commercial meditation studios, a non-commercial social spot for relaxation in the fast-paced IT Capital of India. We do not consider ourselves coaches, gurus, or experts. We are fellow learners, compelled to share what we have found most helpful with a very small circle of like-minded individuals.

Good to Know

Practical Details

🧘

Who It's For

All levels welcome, from complete beginners to advanced practitioners. Any age group: teenagers to seniors. Maximum 5 guests per session to preserve the intimacy of the circle.

🗓

Duration & Timing

2.5 hours total. Doors open 20 minutes before the program starts. Please plan to arrive on time, late entry disrupts the group field established in the opening circle.

👟

Shoes Off Policy

Shoes are removed at the beginning of the session. Please wear socks. Comfortable, flexible clothing is recommended as you will sit on the ground and lie on your back.

📵

Mobile-Free Zone

All guests switch off their phones and keep them in their bags. This is your chance to genuinely unplug. The session is designed for it.

🌿

Energy Exchange

The fee of $25 exists to reduce no-shows and ensure commitment for 2.5 hours. If the session helps you, the real contribution is integrating meditation into your daily life.

🌐

Languages

Hosted in English. Pre-recorded sessions are in Universal Sound and Music, making them accessible beyond language, ideal for international visitors and non-native speakers.

Nothing to prepare. Just come ready to slow down.
